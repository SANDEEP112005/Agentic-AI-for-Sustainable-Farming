Understanding Your Sustainability Score

How the score is calculated:

  • Water Usage (40% weight): Lower water usage = higher score. Target: ≤ 2.0 ML/hectare. Using drip irrigation or rainwater harvesting can improve this.
  • Fertilizer Use (30% weight): Less chemical fertilizer = higher score. Target: ≤ 1.5 tons/hectare. Organic alternatives like vermicompost boost your score.
  • Crop Rotation (30% weight): Practicing rotation gives full marks. It prevents soil depletion and reduces pest buildup naturally.

Score guide: 🟢 80-100 = Excellent | 🟡 60-79 = Good | 🟠 40-59 = Needs improvement | 🔴 0-39 = Critical
